Histogram
Distinct flat regions
This automatically textures the entire map based on the height and gradient of the terrain. The rules are:
1. Terrain below abyssal water is set to base;
2. Terrain below any water is set to layer 1;
3. Terrain just above water is set to layer 1;
4. The number of flat regions is estimated. Flat regions are then assigned layer 2, 3 and 4 as they increase in height;
5. Layer 5 is mixed into all other layers based on gradient to indicate cliffs.

Note that the edges of regions are blurred together automatically based on height.
